FAQ

What is MeBo?

MeBo is an online message board. Users can create private boards, anonymously and for free. Boards can be shared with other users by their unique link or ID. Anyone who know the link to a board can read, write and edit messages on the board.

What can I do on a board?

Basically, users can write message on the board which other users can see. However, users can not only read, but also edit or delete message. Every board is completely collaborative.

Where can I get the code?

You can find the project on GitHub: https://github.com/tuhrig/mebo.
